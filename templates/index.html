eep-Clu

Deep Cluster Interpretation

More Info

Deep-Clu is an application to help you interpret the results of cluster analysis through the use of large language models (LLMs) such as from GPT-4.

The application enables you to enter the members of clusters and their comparative risks, and the expertise required to interpret them (e.g., Clinical Expert). Deep-Clu will use this information to automatically generate an engineered prompt which solicits interpretations from a LLM for each cluster related to labels, mechanisms for co-occurrence and risk for adverse outcomes, and interventions to reduce the risk of those outcomes.

You can review and modify the generated prompt from Deep-Clu, and submit it to GPT-4 for interpretation.
